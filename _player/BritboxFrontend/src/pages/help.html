Can I try it before I subscribe?

Yes

Start your free trial now

If you decide that BritBox isn’t for you – no worries. No commitment. You can cancel anytime.

How much is it?

BritBox offers monthly and annual subscription options. A monthly subscription is $6.99 USD a month in the U.S. and $8.99 CAD in Canada. An annual subscription is $69.99 USD in the U.S. and $89.99 CAD in Canada. Compared to the price of a monthly subscription, an annual subscription offers 12 months for the equivalent price of 10. Additional sales tax may apply in some states. A subscription gives you unlimited access to all BritBox programs including exclusive premieres, hard-to-find gems, national treasures and everyday favorites.

Where is BritBox available?

BritBox.com is currently available in the US and Canada. If you subscribe in the US or Canada, your subscription can be used in either region. In the UK, subscriptions are available at BritBox.co.uk. Subscriptions to BritBox.co.uk can't be used in the US or Canada (and vice versa).

Why are some shows no longer available? When will they be back?

BritBox needs to secure proper licenses for our content and those licenses are granted for a limited period of time. If a license for a show has expired we can often extend it but sometimes we have to remove it from the service. If we don’t have a show you would like to see, please email us at suggestions-us@britbox.com.

In Canada please email help-ca@britbox.com or please call customer service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week at 1-877-425-5443.
All assistance and requests for BritBox UK should be directed to www.britbox.co.uk

Can I watch on multiple devices?

Yes, you can access BritBox from web, Apple TV (4th Generation or Airplay to older models), Roku, iPhone, iPad, Chromecast & Android devices, and Samsung Smart TVs (2015 models onwards).

Do you offer the same shows in the US and Canada?

BritBox needs to secure proper licenses for our content and unfortunately in some cases these licenses are granted for different windows in different territories. We are continuously working hard to bring all of the shows that you love to BritBox in both the U.S. and Canada. If we don’t have a show that you would like to see, please email us at suggestions-us@britbox.com

Can I buy a subscription as a gift?

We offer 1-year gift subscriptions as a great gift for all, whether they're new to BritBox or are already a subscriber.

A 1-year gift subscription to BritBox is $69.99 in the US and $89.99 in Canada (the price difference accounts for differences in exchange rates) and includes 12 months' access to great British telly on BritBox. Compared to a monthly subscription, a 1 year subscription offers 12 months for the equivalent price of 10 months. Monthly subscriptions are not available to gift. Gift subscriptions can't be redeemed on Amazon or Apple TV Channels.

To purchase a 1-year BritBox subscription:
  1. Visit www.britbox.com/gift-purchase and complete the purchase and payment process.
  2. When asked to enter an email address, enter your own email address.
  3. The gift code will be sent via email immediately. If you can't locate it after a few minutes, please check your spam or junk folder.
  4. Share the email with your lucky recipient by printing or forwarding the email. Please keep a copy for your records in case it is misplaced.

The subscription will begin once your recipient redeems their code, not when you purchase it.

How to use BritBox

How do I sign up?

To sign up on web, follow these steps:
  1. Click on “Sign In” or the circular ID Icon in the upper right corner
  2. On the sign in page, Click on the "Start your 7-day FREE trial" button
  3. On the register page, enter your name and email address, create your password, and click the "Register" button
  4. On the payment details page, enter in your credit card information, and click the "Start free trial" button
To sign up on Roku, follow these steps:
  1. Click on the circular ID Icon in the upper right corner
  2. On the next screen, click on the "Register" button
  3. If a "Request for information" prompt appears click "OK"
  4. Enter your email address, create your password, and click the "Register" button
  5. On the successful account creation screen, click the "Subscribe" button
  6. On the next screen, click the "Start Free Trial" button
To sign up on Apple TV, follow these steps:
  1. In the BritBox app for Apple TV, click on the circular ID Icon in the upper right corner
  2. On the next screen, click on the "Register" button
  3. Enter your name, email address, create your password, and click the "Register" button
  4. On the successful account creation screen, click the "Subscribe" button
  5. On the next screen, click the "Start Free Trial" button
To sign up on iOS, follow these steps:
  1. On the homepage of the BritBox iOS app, click on the "Start your 7-day FREE trial" button
  2. On the register page, enter your name and email address, create your password, and click the "Register" button
  3. If a "Confirm Purchase" prompt appears, click "Continue" and your subscription will be billed through your Apple ID default payment method.
  4. If a "Verification Required" prompt or a prompt about your Apple ID payment information, follow Apple's instructions and return to the BritBox app
  5. If a dialogue box does not appear, click the "Subscribe" Button and return to either step 3 or 4
To sign up on Samsung Smart TVs, follow these steps:
  1. On your Samsung TV, install and open the BritBox app. Instructions are available from Samsung HERE
  2. Click the "Sign In" button in the top right.
  3. Visit https://www.britbox.com/connect/samsung in a web browser on your desktop or mobile device.
  4. On the register page, enter your name and email address, create your password, and click the "Register" button
  5. On the payment details page, enter in your credit card information, and click the "Start free trial" button
  6. Enter the unique code displayed on your TV and click "Connect". Once connected, your TV screen will refresh.

How do I redeem a gift subscription?

New Subscribers
  1. Visit www.britbox.com/register and create an account.
  2. On Step 2, select Annual Subscription. Then, under the Gift Subscription tab, enter your gift code into the box and select Apply. Please note that the 7-day free trial does not apply when redeeming a gift subscription.
  3. Enter your credit card details. You will not be charged until your subscription renews after 1 year. Your card details are stored securely and updates may be made at any time by logging in to your account.
  4. Start watching! You can use your newly created account on any supported platform (except for Amazon Channels and Apple TV Channels). Find out more about where to watch BritBox?.
Current Subscribers
  1. Log in to your account at www.britbox.com/account/edit and select Account Details, then select the Subscription tab.
  2. Select the 'Gift subscription' tab at the bottom of the screen. Enter your gift code and Apply.
  3. Your annual gift subscription will start once your current billing cycle ends.
Previous Subscribers
  1. If you were a BritBox subscriber in the past, but are not currently a subscriber, please log in to your account at www.britbox.com/account/edit and select Account Details, then select the Subscription tab.
  2. Select Subscribe. You will then be taken to our payment page.
  3. Select the 'Gift subscription' tab, enter and apply your gift code.
  4. Enter your credit card details. You will not be charged until your subscription renews after 1 year. Your card details are stored securely, updates may be made at any time by logging in to your account.
  5. Your gift subscription will start straight away.

If you currently or have previously used Amazon Channels or Apple TV Channels to subscribe to BritBox, please follow the steps for a New Subscriber above. Unfortunately we are unable to offer gift subscriptions on Amazon or Apple TV Channels.

How does billing work?

The credit card you have on file in your account will be billed automatically each month or annually on the same day as your original subscription, after the initial trial period is over. Your BritBox subscription will automatically renew each month unless you cancel at least 24 hours before the end of your billing cycle.

What tax applies to my subscription?

Sales tax is charged if the zip (or postal code) on your account's payment method is located in a state requires sales tax to be charged on digital video subscriptions. The tax rate is determined by your county or state and varies.

Tax is required in Arkansas, Arizona, Colorado, Connecticut, Florida, Iowa, Kansas, Kentucky, Maine, Minnesota, Nebraska, New Mexico, North Carolina, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Rhode Island, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas, Vermont, Washington, Wisconsin, Quebec and Sasketchewan.

You can update the billing zip on your BritBox account by logging in to your account and updating your payment method's zip or postal code. Changes are effective from the next subscription renewal.

How do I access closed captioning?

To access on Web, Samsung TVs, iOS, or Android:
  1. If the episode or movie is playing and you can see other elements of the video player like the volume slider and progress bar but an "S" or “CC” icon does not appear on the lower right hand side of the video player, then closed captioning is unavailable for this episode or movie.
  2. If the episode or movie is playing and you cannot see any elements of the video player like the volume slider and progress bar, see steps 3 and 4.
  3. If you are using an Android or iOS device, tap the screen and proceed to step 5.
  4. If you are using a PC, move the cursor and proceed to step 5.
  5. If closed captioning is available for an episode or movie, either an "S" or “CC” icon will appear on the lower right hand side of the video player.
  6. If a light blue bar isn't below the "S" or “CC” icon, click on the “CC” icon to turn on closed captioning.
  7. If a light blue bar appears below the "S" or “CC” icon then closed captioning is already turned on for the episode or movie. Click on the "s" or “CC” icon to turn it off.
To Access on Roku or Samsung TVs:
  1. First click the down arrow on the TV remote while the episode or movie is playing.
  2. If closed captioning is available for a show, a “CC” icon will appear on the lower right hand side of the video player.
  3. Click on the “CC” icon to turn on closed captioning.
  4. Click on “CC” again to turn it off.
To Access on Apple TV:
  1. Navigate to the image of the episode you would like to watch on its brand or season page. For a movie, navigate to the movie's specific landing page.
  2. If closed captioning is available for a show, a grey button with a play icon and the letters "CC" on it will appear below the episode image or beside a movie's default play buttons.
  3. Instead of clicking on the episode image itself, nagivate down to the grey button with a play icon and the letters "CC" on it. The button will highlight white when you reach it.
  4. Click this button to play the movie or episode with closed captioning.
  5. To turn off closed captioning for an episode, navigate back to the image of the episode you would like to watch on a show's brand or season page and click the image.
  6. To turn off closed captioning for a movie, navigate to the movie's specific landing page and select the default play buttons.

Our service meets the FCC requirements for closed captioning and we aim to caption all of our content soon.

If I am a BritBox subscriber can I access my subscription on Amazon or Apple TV Channels?

Unfortunately, you can't use your BritBox login and password to access the service on Amazon or Apple TV Channels. Amazon and Apple Channels are separate services that currently do not support this. We will provide an update when this is supported.

If I signed up for BritBox on Amazon Channels or Apple TV Channels, can I access the Britbox website or apps using my login or password?

If you signed up via Amazon Channels, you'll only be able to watch through Amazon's products. For Apple, you may only watch through Apple TV Channels if you subscribe using Apple TV Channels, please note that this is a separate subscription option to a direct subscription to BritBox, paid using an Apple iTunes subscription account.

If I subscribe to BritBox in the US will it work when I travel to Canada?

Your BritBox.com subscription can be used in both the US and Canada, however show availability may differ in each region.

If I subscribe to BritBox in the US or Canada will it work when I travel to the UK or Europe?

Your BritBox.com subscription can be used in both the US and Canada, however it doesn't provide access to BritBox.co.uk or to view content outside of North America.
